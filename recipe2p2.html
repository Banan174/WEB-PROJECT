How to brew Hario V60


STEP 1 :
Put filter paper into your V60, and place it on top of your mug or server. Rinse the paper with hot water, then discard the water.
STEP 2 :
Grind 15g of coffee and place it in the filter.
STEP 3 :
Gently tap the V60 on the bench to settle the grounds into a flat bed.
STEP 4 :
Stir to make sure all the grounds are wet, and wait 30 seconds.
STEP 5 :
Gently pour in more water until you’ve added a total of 250ml. Pour using a circular motion.
STEP 6 :
Gently stir again once the pour is complete.
STEP 7 :
Swirl your server or mug, then enjoy!
