Steps to make Simple Layered Fruit Mocktails


STEP 1 :
Make Crushes using fruit and sugar and blend it without adding water.
STEP 2 :
Slowly layer it with thin consistency (the densest) fruit juice in to a tall glass to make the layered Mocktail
